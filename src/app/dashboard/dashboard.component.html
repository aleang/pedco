The Trip of a Lifetime

So you’ve heard about 2 guys that wanted to cycle from China to Portugal. Pheng & Matt are from New Zealand and have been planning this trip for several years. As a warm up, in 2012 they toured from Bangkok to Hanoi travelling 3000 km over 2 months. This experience made them hungry for an even longer, wilder, and more rewarding trip.

Where are we heading?

Starting at the eastern Chinese city of Tiānjīn, we cycled the Silk Road arriving in Iran, then sip lots of teas around the Middle East and continued through Europe to the Atlantic’s coastal city of Lisbon.
